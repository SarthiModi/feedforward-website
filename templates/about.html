About Us

Feed Forward is built on a simple yet powerful mission — to connect those with surplus food to those who need it the most. We partner with restaurants, donors, and NGOs to ensure no food goes to waste, turning compassion into action and hunger into hope.

Our Vision

At Feed Forward, our vision is simple — to reduce food waste and fight hunger. We aim to empower communities to share surplus food efficiently and responsibly, ensuring that every meal cooked can help feed someone in need. Together, we can make a difference — one plate at a time. 🍽
